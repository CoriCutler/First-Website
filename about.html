About Us

Description

Journey to the past is about a group of aliens that are exploring mysterious worlds to learn about other cultures and resources, while exploring they crash land on this mysterious planet and they need to collect special parts found all across the world to repair their ship are return home. while exploring this world they find other creatures and most of them are hostile.

Level Design

The levels are designed so everyone can understand how the game works and how to play the level. The levels gradually get more difficult until the player has to use all there mental capacity to progress. The levels are very bright to make everything clear and easy to see also all the graphics change mattering on what environment the player is in.

Collectables

Throughout the levels there are different types of collectables. The most basic collectable is coins, there are loads of coins throughout the level and the player obtains 1 point every time they collect a coin. Another collect able is special gems that give the player an upgrade point which the player can use to upgrade their statistics. The final collectable is the ship part which completes the level, there is only one ship part per level and once the player collects the part, the level finishes.

Players

There are three players through out the game, the player can select which player they want to use for the level, each player has different attributes this can help the player complete the level but it matters on how the player plays the game, all the level are designed to be able to be completed with all the characters.
